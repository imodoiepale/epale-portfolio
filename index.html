About Me

Every great system begins with a problem worth solving

I'm James Epale — an AI Engineer and CTO based in Nairobi, Kenya. I lead NSAIT (Nairobi Space of Artificial Intelligence Tools), where we build production-grade AI systems for government agencies and enterprises across East Africa.

My work sits at the intersection of AI engineering, systems architecture, and real-world business automation. I've built everything from intelligent document processing pipelines handling 15,000+ documents monthly, to wildlife conservation AI serving 200+ field officers across 23 national parks.

I don't just build demos — I ship production systems. My E-FNS Immigration Automation monitors Kenya's immigration portal 24/7, generating PDFs and WhatsApp alerts in real-time. My KRA Tax Automation Suite eliminated 94% of calculation errors for 200+ organizations. CareerLaunch, my latest venture, is tackling youth unemployment across Africa with AI-powered job application automation.

I'm quietly confident, endlessly curious, and driven by the belief that well-architected AI can solve Africa's most pressing challenges — one system at a time.
